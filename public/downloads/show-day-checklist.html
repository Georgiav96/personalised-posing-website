Your Ultimate Show Day Packing Checklist

Everything You Need for a Stress-Free Comp Day

From one competitor to another: it's always better to be over-prepared than under-prepared! Pack your comp day bag a few days early to avoid stress, double-check everything, and feel stage-ready with confidence.

» Stage Essentials «

Main Stagewear

  • Competition bikini or costume (double-check all connectors!)
  • Federation-approved heels
  • Jewellery (earrings, bracelet, ring)
  • Competition number & safety pins

Secure & Stay-Put Items

  • Bikini Bite or fabric glue (to help keep costume in place)
  • Safety pins (for number or emergency fastening)

Back-Up Options

  • Spare connectors
  • Back-up bikini or costume (if possible)
  • Back-up heels (if possible)

💡 Pro tip: Pack a mini pump-up bag with essentials like pump bands, tan/hair/makeup touch-ups, and pre-stage snacks so you're not running back and forth between your main bag and the stage area.

» Backstage & Comfort Essentials «

Pre-Stage Wear & Essentials

  • Tan suit (worn to/from tan and while top coat dries)
  • Robe (to wear over bikini while waiting backstage)
  • Slippers (easy to slip on, tan-safe)
  • Pillow & blanket (stay comfy during the backstage wait)
  • Resistance bands or light dumbbells (for pump-up)

Post-Stage Comfort

  • Comfy post-show clothes (dark/tan-safe)

Organisation & Entertainment

  • Clear zip bags or packing cubes (to organise outfits, jewellery, or makeup by stage order or category)
  • Calming tools (book, podcast, journal, etc)
  • Headphones or earphones
  • Phone charger or portable power bank

💡 Pro tip: Bring an umbrella in case it rains when walking from your car to the venue — it's a simple way to protect your tan from water spots before stepping on stage.

» Food, Fuel & Hydration «

Pre-Stage Fuel

  • Meal prep & carb-up snacks (as per coach's instructions)
  • Esky or insulated lunch bag (to keep meals fresh)
  • Food scales (to weigh out food accurately)

Hydration

  • Water bottle (non-glass)
  • Electrolytes or hydration tablets

Utensils & Meal Tools

  • Reusable straw (to protect lipstick/avoid smudging)
  • Cutlery, container or plate, and napkins

Post-Show Recovery

  • Post-comp meal (prepped in advance)

📝 Note: Shows can sometimes run late or get pushed back unexpectedly. It's smart to bring extra food (as per your coach's advice) to ensure you stay fuelled and focused right up until your time on stage.
