Your Ultimate Show Day Packing Checklist

Everything you need for a stress-free comp day

» Stage Essentials «

Main Stagewear

  • Competition bikini/costume (check connectors!)
  • Federation-approved heels
  • Jewellery (earrings, bracelet, ring)
  • Competition number & safety pins

Secure & Back-Up

  • Bikini Bite or fabric glue
  • Safety pins
  • Spare connectors
  • Back-up bikini (if possible)
  • Back-up heels (if possible)
» Backstage & Comfort «

Pre-Stage Wear

  • Tan suit
  • Robe
  • Slippers (tan-safe)
  • Pillow & blanket
  • Resistance bands/dumbbells

Organisation

  • Clear zip bags/packing cubes
  • Calming tools (book, podcast)
  • Headphones
  • Phone charger/power bank
  • Comfy post-show clothes
💡 Pro tip: Pack a mini pump-up bag with essentials so you're not running back and forth.
» Food, Fuel & Hydration «

Pre-Stage Fuel

  • Meal prep & snacks
  • Esky/insulated bag
  • Food scales

Hydration

  • Water bottle
  • Electrolytes
  • Reusable straw

Utensils & Post

  • Cutlery & napkins
  • Post-comp meal
» Toiletries & Touch-Ups «

Hair

  • Brush or comb
  • Bobby pins & hair ties
  • Hairspray & shine spray
  • Lint roller

Makeup

  • Lipstick
  • Translucent powder
  • Compact mirror
  • Lash glue & tweezers
  • Makeup wipes

Freshness

  • Tan-safe deodorant
  • Dryer sheets
  • Perfume (tan-safe)

Misc Touch-Ups

  • Nail file/glue
  • Tanning glove/roller
  • Sewing kit
  • Handheld mirror
» First Aid & Sanitary «

Hygiene

  • Portable pee cup
  • Tampons/pads
  • Cotton pads & tissues

Pain Relief

  • Panadol/Nurofen
  • Antihistamines

Medical Fixes

  • Bandaids & blister pads
  • Tweezers & scissors
💡 Pro tip: Bring an umbrella — protect your tan from rain when walking from car to venue!
