Your Ultimate
Show Day Packing Checklist

Everything you need for a stress-free comp day

From one competitor to another: it's always better to be over-prepared than under-prepared! Pack your comp day bag a few days early to avoid stress, double-check everything, and feel stage-ready with confidence.

» Stage Essentials «

Main Stagewear

  • Competition bikini or costume (double-check all connectors!)
  • Federation-approved heels
  • Jewellery (earrings, bracelet, ring)
  • Competition number & safety pins

Secure & Stay-Put Items

  • Bikini Bite or fabric glue
  • Safety pins (for number or emergency)

Back-Up Options

  • Spare connectors
  • Back-up bikini (if possible)
  • Back-up heels (if possible)

💡 Pro tip: Pack a mini pump-up bag with essentials like pump bands, tan/hair/makeup touch-ups, and pre-stage snacks so you're not running back and forth between your main bag and the stage area.

» Backstage & Comfort Essentials «

Pre-Stage Wear

  • Tan suit (worn to/from tan)
  • Robe (to wear backstage)
  • Slippers (easy to slip on, tan-safe)
  • Pillow & blanket
  • Resistance bands or dumbbells

Post-Stage Comfort

  • Comfy post-show clothes (dark/tan-safe)

Organisation

  • Clear zip bags or packing cubes
  • Calming tools (book, podcast, journal)
  • Headphones or earphones
  • Phone charger / power bank

💡 Pro tip: Bring an umbrella in case it rains when walking from your car to the venue — it's a simple way to protect your tan from water spots before stepping on stage.

» Food, Fuel & Hydration «

Pre-Stage Fuel

  • Meal prep & carb-up snacks
  • Esky or insulated lunch bag
  • Food scales

Hydration

  • Water bottle (non-glass)
  • Electrolytes or hydration tablets

Utensils & Meal Tools

  • Reusable straw (protect lipstick)
  • Cutlery, container, napkins

Post-Show

  • Post-comp meal (prepped in advance)
» Toiletries & Touch-Ups «

Hair Essentials

  • Brush or comb
  • Bobby pins & hair ties
  • Hairspray & shine spray
  • Lint roller (optional)

Makeup Essentials

  • Lipstick (touch-ups)
  • Translucent powder & blotting paper
  • Compact mirror
  • Lash glue & tweezers
  • Makeup wipes or baby wipes

Freshness on Show Day

  • Tan-safe deodorant (aluminium-free)
  • Dryer sheets (discreet freshness)
  • Perfume (tan-safe, apply lightly)

Miscellaneous

  • Nail file or nail glue
  • Tanning glove or roller
  • Small sewing kit
  • Small handheld mirror
» First Aid & Sanitary «

Toileting & Hygiene

  • Portable pee cup or funnel
  • Tampons, pads, or liners
  • Cotton pads & tissues

Pain & Medical

  • Pain relief (Panadol, Nurofen)
  • Antihistamines
  • Bandaids & blister pads
  • Tweezers, scissors, safety pins
